Phishing Protection Tips

Learn how to recognize and avoid phishing attacks

Inspect URLs Carefully

Hover over links before clicking. Check for misspellings like "amaz0n.com" or unusual domains.

Beware of Urgency

Phishers create artificial deadlines. Legitimate organizations rarely demand immediate action.

Verify Sender Identity

Contact organizations through official channels, not links in suspicious emails.

Enable MFA

Multi-factor authentication protects accounts even if credentials are compromised.

Avoid Unknown Attachments

Don't open attachments from unknown senders. They may contain malware.

Stay Informed

Regular security training helps you recognize evolving phishing tactics.
